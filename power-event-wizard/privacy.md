# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 9, 2026

## Overview

Power Event Wizard is a macOS application that helps you manage system power events. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## What Data We Collect

The application itself does not collect any personal data. It simply provides a graphical interface to manage existing macOS power settings using the native `pmset` command.

**No data is sent to our servers.** All operations occur locally on your computer.

## How We Use Your Data

Power Event Wizard does not:
- Send any information to external servers
- Track your usage
- Collect system information
- Store any personal data

## Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored externally, your privacy is protected by your local macOS security settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
