Last updated: January 9, 2026

This policy explains how iBoot handles data. The app is designed to avoid collecting, storing, or sharing personal information.

## What we collect

- We do not collect personal data.
- We do not track analytics, usage, or device identifiers.
- We do not use third-party SDKs for analytics, ads, or profiling.

## Local operations

- iBoot runs locally to open the macOS Startup Disk panel.
- No data leaves your device.
- No accounts or cloud services are used.

## Support

If you contact us, we will see the information you share in that message. We only use it to respond to your request.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date.
