**Last Updated: April 17, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Privacy

SubWin is designed to run entirely on your Mac. We do not operate any servers and have no way to collect your data.

## Data Collection

**We collect ZERO personal data.** Specifically:

- No account registration required
- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting
- No advertising networks
- No third-party SDKs

## Data Storage

All data is stored **exclusively on your Mac**:

- Your Windows virtual machine lives in your app sandbox
- No cloud synchronization
- No server uploads

## Network Access

SubWin itself makes no network connections to our servers. The only network activity comes from:

- **Apple's App Store** — for purchase verification (handled entirely by Apple)
- **Your Windows VM** — which accesses the internet through your Mac's network, just like any app you run
- **Remote Desktop** — connections are local only (`localhost:3389`), between your Mac and the VM running on the same machine

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to:

- Request access to
- Request deletion of
- Request correction of

Your data never leaves your Mac, so you have complete control.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date above.
